Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which Desinerz Academy processes refund requests and handles cancellations for our workshops, courses, and other paid offerings. Please read this carefully before making a purchase.

Important Notice — Workshop Purchases

All workshop registrations are final and non-cancellable once payment is confirmed. By completing your purchase, you agree that no cancellation or refund will be issued for workshop bookings under any circumstances.

01

Workshop Registrations — No Cancellation

Once you have successfully purchased a seat in any Desinerz Academy workshop, your registration is confirmed and non-refundable. Cancellations are not accepted after payment, regardless of the reason — including scheduling conflicts, change of plans, or personal emergencies. We encourage you to review all workshop details carefully before purchasing.

02

Workshop Rescheduling

While cancellations are not permitted, if you are unable to attend a workshop you have registered for, you may write to us at hello@desinerz.com at least 48 hours before the event to request a one-time transfer of your seat to the next available batch of the same workshop, subject to availability. Seat transfers are not guaranteed and are offered at our sole discretion.

03

Workshop Cancelled by Desinerz Academy

In the rare event that Desinerz Academy cancels a workshop, all registered participants will receive a full refund within 7–10 business days, or the option to transfer their seat to a future batch. We will notify all affected participants via email as soon as a cancellation decision is made.

04

Non-Refundable Items

The following are strictly non-refundable under all circumstances:

  • → Workshop registrations (once purchased)
  • → Digital downloads and design resources
  • → Any course or product purchased during a sale or at a discounted price
  • → Courses where the 7-day refund window has expired
05

Contact Us

For refund requests, questions, or concerns regarding this policy, please reach out to our support team at hello@desinerz.com . We aim to respond within 2 business days.
